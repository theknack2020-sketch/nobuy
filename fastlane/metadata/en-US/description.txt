One question every day: Did you spend money today?

NoBuy Day is the simplest way to track your discretionary spending. No complex budget categories - just tap "No Spend Today" and watch your saving streak grow.

FEATURES
- One-tap daily logging - done in 2 seconds
- Streak system - track consecutive no-spend days
- Calendar view - see your month at a glance, green and red days
- Monthly summary - see your progress as a percentage
- Mandatory expense separation - rent and bills don't break your streak
- Motivational notifications - celebrate streak milestones

PERFECT FOR
- No Buy Challenge participants
- Anyone reducing unnecessary spending
- Minimalist lifestyle followers
- People saving for a goal
- Breaking impulse shopping habits

WHY NOBUY DAY?
Unlike other finance apps, NoBuy Day doesn't ask for complex categories, bank connections, or detailed entries. It asks one question: Did you make an unnecessary purchase today?

The power of this simple question is proven - research shows spending awareness can increase savings by up to 20%.

PRIVACY
Your data stays on your device only. No account required, no data collected.
