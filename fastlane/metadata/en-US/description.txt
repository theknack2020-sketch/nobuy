Take control of your money — one day at a time. NoBuy is the simplest no-spend tracker that helps you break impulse buying habits, build saving streaks, and become more intentional with every purchase decision.

Join thousands who are embracing the no-buy challenge and underconsumption trend. Track each day with a single tap, watch your streak grow, and see real progress toward financial freedom.

HOW IT WORKS

Tap "No Spend Today" at the end of each day
Log if you made a discretionary purchase
Mark mandatory expenses (rent, bills) — they don't break your streak

FEATURES

Streak Tracker — Build consecutive no-spend day streaks and celebrate milestones
Calendar View — See your month at a glance with color-coded days
Monthly Stats — Track your no-spend percentage and monthly progress
Achievements — Unlock badges at 3, 7, 14, 30, 60, and 100-day milestones
Mandatory Expenses — Separate rent, bills, and groceries so they never break your streak
Smart Reminders — Evening notifications to log your day and protect your streak

Impulse Buy Checklist — Answer "Should I buy this?" questions before any purchase to make smarter decisions
Custom Challenges — Set your own no-spend goals (7 days, 30 days, or any duration you choose)
Data Export — Download your history as CSV (Pro)
Daily Tips — Research-backed spending awareness tips to stay motivated

WHY NOBUY WORKS

Unlike complex budget apps, NoBuy asks one powerful question each day: Did you make an unnecessary purchase today?

Research shows that spending awareness alone can increase savings by up to 20%. NoBuy turns that awareness into a daily habit with streaks, milestones, and achievements that keep you motivated.

No spreadsheets. No bank connections. No complicated categories. Just one tap, every day.
