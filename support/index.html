Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a no-spend day?

A day where you didn't make any discretionary purchases. Essential expenses (rent, bills, groceries) don't break your streak — you can mark them as "Essential Only."

What is Streak Freeze?

Streak Freeze protects your streak for one day when you make a discretionary purchase. Free users get 1 freeze per month, Pro users get unlimited.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → tap "Restore Purchase." Your Pro status will be restored if you previously purchased NoBuy Pro on the same Apple ID.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device only. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed.

Can I export my data?

Yes! NoBuy Pro users can export their data as a CSV file from Settings → Export Data.

How do I cancel notifications?

Go to Settings → Reminders → toggle off Daily Reminder or Streak Notifications.
