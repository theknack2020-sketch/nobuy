Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

NoBuy ("the App") is developed by TheKnack. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All your spending records, streaks, achievements, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is transmitted to any server.

No Accounts Required

NoBuy does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or usage statistics.

In-App Purchases

NoBuy offers a one-time in-app purchase ("NoBuy Pro") processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all transactions.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

NoBuy does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at theknack2020@gmail.com.
