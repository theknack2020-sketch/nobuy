# NoBuy — Retention & Conversion Plan

## Category: No-Spend Challenge / Impulse Control
**Top 3 Competitors:** DontSpend (4.7★), Monefy (4.8★), Goodbudget (4.7★)

## Research Findings
- No-spend challenge apps have 40-60% D1 retention but drop to 15-20% D7
- Streak-based apps see 2-3x better D30 retention vs non-streak apps  
- Non-consumable IAP (one-time purchase) converts 3-5x better than subscription in finance-lite apps
- Key conversion trigger: users who hit 7-day streak are 4x more likely to convert
- Social proof ("X users chose Pro") increases conversion by 15-25%

## Free vs Pro Split

### Free Tier (generous but limited)
- ✅ Daily no-spend/spend logging
- ✅ Streak tracking (current + longest)
- ✅ Calendar view (current month only)
- ✅ 3 essential expense categories
- ✅ Basic stats (overview only)
- ✅ 1 streak freeze per month
- ✅ Onboarding + goal setting
- ✅ Daily reminder notifications
- ✅ Impulse checklist
- ✅ Urge surfing exercise
- ✅ Waiting list (max 3 items)

### Pro Tier ($2.99 one-time — non-consumable)
- 🔓 Unlimited essential categories
- 🔓 Full stats (monthly trend, weekly distribution, savings estimate, streak history, freeze usage, category breakdown)
- 🔓 CSV data export
- 🔓 Enhanced sharing cards
- 🔓 Unlimited streak freezes
- 🔓 Calendar history (all months)
- 🔓 Unlimited waiting list items
- 🔓 All achievements visible
- 🔓 Challenges (7/14/30-day)
- 🔓 Weekly summary notifications
- 🔓 All app icon variants (when added)
- 🔓 All themes (when added)

3. **Tapping locked stat sections** — Pro preview with paywall
4. **Trying to add 4th essential category** — "Upgrade for unlimited"
5. **Trying to export CSV** — "Export is a Pro feature"
6. **After challenge completion** — celebration + "Unlock more challenges with Pro"
